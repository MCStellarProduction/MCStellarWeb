A year and four months after the first edition, the terrifying Backrooms arrived in Minecraft as part of an event by Eufonia Studio on September 7, 2024.

Inspired by an urban legend or creepypasta, this event recreated fear, anxiety, and anguish, attracting 100 content creators. The collaboration of Soarinng and Kori Kimura brought a chilling setting to life, where AI-programmed entities generated a terrifying and unique experience for each participant.

Entidad Desconocida

This snake-like entity will detect and pursue you if you make noise or enter its line of sight. It can submerge and emerge from the water.

Dullers

Shadowy entities that absorb the light around them, creating areas of impenetrable darkness. They move stealthily and often attack their victims by surprise.

Faceling Hombre

Facelings are faceless humans with smooth skin and no distinguishing features where their face should be.
